Papa's Spaghetti

Recipe

The perfect thing to SPICE up your week

This is one of those family recipes I rarely share with others, but now it's finally time to share the pure AWESOMENESS of this spicy tomato sauce my Grandfather perfected to his taste pallet together with additions from the rest of the family. it's easy and farely quick to make, but don't wear your best whites cus it's gonna get saucyyy!

Ingredients

Makes sauce for half a kilo of Spaghetti

    2 Onions
    2 Cloves of Garlic
    1 dl Olive Oil
    700 g Groundbeef
    6 dl Tomato juice
    3 1/2 dl Tomato puree
    1 can Tomato paste
    4 hot Chili peppers
    1 teaspoon Salt
    1/2 teaspoon Pepper
    1 teaspoon Sugar
    12 Stuffed green Olives sliced

Steps

  1. Peal Onions and Garlic.
  2. Slice onions and mash the garlic.
  3. Brown onions and garlic in oil in a deep pot.
  4. Add meat and brown slowly.
  5. Add tomato juice, puree and paste as well as the chili peppers, salt, pepper and sugar.
  6. Let the sauce simmer for 2 1/2 to 3 hours until thick.
  7. Remove chili peppers and add the sliced olives.
  8. Let simmer for another 10 minutes or longer.
  9. Serve with spaghetti, topped with grated parmesan cheese and garlic bread.
  10. Enjoy!
