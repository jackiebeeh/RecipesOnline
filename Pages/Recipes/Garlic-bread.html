Garlic Bread

Recipe

The perfect side dish for the ultimate spaghetti sauce show down!

Ingredients

    White bread loaf
    2 cloves of Garlic
    Olive oil and/or Butter
    Salt
    Pepper
    Oregano or other herbs from the garden

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 C.
  2. Peal and mash garlic and cut into tiny pieces.
  3. Mix Ingredients in a bowl
  4. Butter every slice of bread and sprinkle the ingredients on top.
  5. Re-connect pieces together and bumndle them up in aluminium foil.
  6. Heat in oven until butter melts and smell of the herbs hits your nose the instant you open the oven.
