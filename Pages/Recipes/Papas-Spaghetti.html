Papa's Spaghetti
Home Garlic Bread How to: Spaghetti
"The perfect thing to SPICE up your week"
This is one of those family recipes I rarely share with others, but now it's finally time to share the pure AWESOMENESS of this spicy tomato sauce my Grandfather perfected to his taste pallet together with additions from the rest of the family. it's easy and farely quick to make, but don't wear your best whites cus it's gonna get sauceyyy!
Ingredients
Makes sauce for half a kilo of Spaghetti
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 dl Olive Oil
  • 700 g Ground beef
  • 6 dl Tomato juice
  • 3 1/2 dl Tomato puree
  • 1 can Tomato paste
  • 4 hot Chili peppers
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Sugar
  • 12 Stuffed green Olives sliced
    • Steps
  • Peal Onions and Garlic.
  • Slice onions and mash the garlic.
  • Brown onions and garlic in oil in a deep pot.
  • Add meat and brown slowly.
  • Add tomato juice, puree and paste as well as the chili peppers, salt, pepper and sugar.
  • Let the sauce simmer for 2 1/2 to 3 hours until thick.
  • Remove chili peppers and add the sliced olives.
  • Let simmer for another 10 minutes or longer.
  • Serve with spaghetti, topped with grated parmesan cheese and garlic bread.
  • Enjoy!
